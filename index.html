Swipr

Matching hungry singles in your area! Swipr is an Android social application to match UCLA students who are buying or selling dining hall swipes on a secondary market.

Tempol

Web app developed using JavaScript and Flask. Using a beat detection and audio conversion libraries, Tempol creates a playlist of songs at a specific tempo. If the original song is too fast or slow, Tempol will convert it to any desired tempo and add it to a playlist. Developed to aid practicing musicians that enjoy along with their favorite artists.

Professor Review

Web scraping application that uses different teacher rating websites (BruinWalk, RateMyProfessors) to find the most common tags for UCLA professors, including difficulty, kindness, and course value.

CNN-Powered Image Registration

Adaptation of Convolutional Neural Network Machine Learning model for image registration of cancer slide images. Our team was able to successfully rotate different tissue cell images to a uniform axis, reducing a large amount of overhead in the image processing pipeline.

Movie Rating Predictor

Using Data Mining Techniques (AdaBoost, SVD) on IMDb information and user ratings to predict how individuals will rate unseen movies. Predicted score out of 10 with a Root Mean Squared Error of 0.81.
